Funding

NPQs are currently fully funded by the Department for Education (DfE) if you work in a:

  • state-funded school in England
  • state-funded organisation that offers 16 to 19 places in England

If you work in early years you may also be eligible. Check your funding eligibility for the Early years leadership NPQ.

Funding is currently available for the 2022 to 2023 and 2023 to 2024 academic years.

You can do more than one NPQ, but will only be eligible for funding for each NPQ once. If you withdraw or fail your course you cannot get funded again for the same NPQ.
