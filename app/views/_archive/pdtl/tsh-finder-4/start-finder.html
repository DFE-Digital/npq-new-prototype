Find an NPQ provider near you

Once you’ve chosen a national professional qualification (NPQ), use this service to find a provider offering your NPQ.

There are 2 types of providers who work together to offer NPQs:

  • lead providers
  • delivery partners

This service finds delivery partners near you and shows you who they partner with (their lead provider).

When you register for an NPQ, you’ll need to know the name of your lead provider.
