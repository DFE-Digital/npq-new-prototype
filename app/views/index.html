Navigate the prototype

The password for the Get an Identity prototype (i.e. the password you're asked for after clicking "Start now") is: gai

Returning users

Use existing@email.com when asked for your email address.

New user attempting to log in

Use new@email.com when asked for your email address.

Private school journey

Type ‘private’ when asked for the name of the school.

School or early years in eligibility check

Type 'not-pp50' when asked for the name of the school or the Ofsted URN.

This will show the funding outcome (not eligible) for a workplace not in the eligibility list.

Entering anything else in those questions, shows the outcome for the workplace in the list (normally eligible, but subject to other rules).
