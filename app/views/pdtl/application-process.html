Apply for an NPQ

Find out how to apply for a national professional qualification (NPQ) or the early headship coaching offer.

  1. Step 1: Choose an NPQ

      Choose from the following NPQs:

      • Leading teaching
      • Leading behaviour and culture
      • Leading literacy
      • Leading teacher development
      • Senior leadership
      • Headship
      • Executive leadership
      • Early years leadership

      If you’re in your first 5 years of headship, you may also be eligible for the early headship coaching offer.

      If you’re employed, talk to your employer or line manager about doing an NPQ. They may have a view on which NPQ you choose, and you’ll need their approval to do an NPQ.

  2. Step 2: Find a provider

    1. If you’re employed, ask your employer or line manager if they have a recommended provider for your training.

      If you’re a teacher and your workplace cannot help, you can find a teaching school hub near you for advice.

      Teaching school hubs partner with providers to offer NPQs. They’ll be able to tell you whether they offer your chosen NPQ and who the provider is.

      Even if they do not offer your chosen NPQ, they can still give advice on other providers.

      For an overview of the different providers offering each NPQ, visit the course pages outlined in step one.

  3. Step 3: Register with the Department for Education

    You need a provisional driving licence to take lessons or practice.
