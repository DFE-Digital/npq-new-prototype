Course funding

Full DfE funding, with no cost to the participant, is available to staff from:

  • state-funded schools in England
  • state-funded organisations that offer 16 to 19 places in England

Early years leadership NPQ

Additional funding is available for early years teachers applying for the early years leadership NPQ (NPQEYL). Find out more in the NPQEYL page.
