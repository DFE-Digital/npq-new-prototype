Funding

NPQs are fully funded by the Department for Education (DfE) if you work in a:

  • state-funded school in England
  • state-funded organisation that offers 16 to 19 places in England

If you work in early years you may also be eligible. Check your funding eligiblty for the Early years leadership NPQ.
