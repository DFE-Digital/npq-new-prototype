Register for a national professional qualification (NPQ)

Use this service to:

  • register for an NPQ or the Early headship coaching offer
  • check or change your registration details if you already registered

You also need to apply separately with your training provider. They’ll contact you once you’ve registered.

Before you start

You need to know:

  • which NPQ you want to do
  • who your provider is

If you have not chosen yet, find out about NPQs and providers.

If you work in early years or childcare, check if your workplace is registered with Ofsted and get their unique reference number (URN) if they have one.

You’ll be asked to create or sign in to your DfE intentity account. We’ll show you how to do this.
