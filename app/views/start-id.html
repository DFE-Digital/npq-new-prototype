Register for a national professional qualification (NPQ)

Use this service to:

  • register for an NPQ or the Early headship coaching offer
  • view a previous registration
  • view the outcome of completed courses

You also need to apply separately with your training provider. They’ll contact you once you’ve registered.

Before you start

You need to know:

  • which NPQ you want to do
  • who your provider is

If you have not chosen an NPQ or provider yet, learn about NPQs.

If you work in early years or childcare, check if your workplace is registered with Ofsted and get their Ofsted unique reference number (URN) if they have one.

Check if you have a teacher reference number (TRN) using Find a lost TRN. You need a TRN to register for an NPQ, even if you’re not a teacher. If you do not have one, request a TRN.

You’ll be asked to create or sign in to your DfE Identity account. We’ll show you how to do this.
