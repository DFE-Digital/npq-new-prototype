Teacher reference number (TRN)

You need a TRN to register for a NPQ, even if you're not a teacher.​​

If you do not have a TRN, you can:​

  • find a lost TRN​
  • request one

If you work in early years, further education, or qualified as a teacher outside England, you may need to request a TRN if you do not already have one.
