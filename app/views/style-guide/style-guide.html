Style guide

The terms we refer to, and how we use them.

National professional qualifications (NPQs)

We refer to the following NPQs:

  • Leading behaviour and culture
  • Leading literacy
  • Leading teaching
  • Leading teacher development
  • Senior leadership
  • Headship
  • Executive leadership
  • Early years leadership

When they‘re in a list, as above, you do not need to repeat ‘NPQ’. Just make sure you introduce them as NPQs.

Example of a list of NPQs on GOV.UK.

Example of a list of NPQs as radio buttons.

However, when in a sentence, you should add ‘NPQ’ at the end.

For example:

Register for the Headship NPQ.

Spell out NPQ on first mention, in lower case.

For example:

National professional qualifications (NPQs) support the professional development of teachers and leaders.

Do not use the NPQ acronyms such as ‘NPQEYL’.

Early headship coaching offer

Headteachers in their first 5 years of headship can get structured support and networking opportunities through the Early headship coaching offer.

Example of how we refer to the Early headship coaching offer.

Scholarship funding

Scholarship funding is provided by the Department for Education (DfE). If someone is eligible for scholarship funding, it means they do not have to pay for their NPQ.

Example of how we refer to scholarship funding.

Do not refer to scholarship funding as ‘DfE funding’, because ‘DfE funding’ could also mean targeted support funding (see below). However, you can specify that it’s provided by DfE.

Targeted support funding (TSF) – Pre April 2024 only

Targeted support funding was provided by DfE before April 2024. If someone was eligible for targeted support funding, it meant their workplace recieved money to help enable their participation. TSF varied based on the school phase and size, up to £800.

The prototype and live service shows details of the targeted support funding participants received. This is only shown in the account area on the registration details pages. Nothing is shown if the participant did not receive the funding or registered after April 2024.
