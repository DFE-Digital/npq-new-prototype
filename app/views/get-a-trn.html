Request a teacher reference number (TRN) for a national professional qualification (NPQ)

You need a TRN to do an NPQ, even if you’re not a teacher.

If you do not have one, you can request one.

Before you request one, check if you have one already using Find a lost TRN.

For example, you’ll have a TRN if you have qualified teacher status (QTS) or early years teacher status (EYTS).

Request a TRN

To request a TRN you need to send a secure email to the Department for Education (DfE) using Galaxkey, including some personal details and proof of identity:

  1. Create a Galaxkey account.
  2. Compose an email in Galaxkey to qts.enquiries@education.gov.uk with the subject line ‘Request TRN for NPQ’.
  3. Include the following personal details in body of the email:
    • full legal name
    • date of birth
    • email address – DfE will respond to this email address with your TRN
  4. Attach a photo or scanned copy of your proof of identity. You can use one of the following:
    • passport
    • driving licence (full or provisional)
    • certificate of residence
    • birth certificate

Once you’ve sent the email, DfE will respond within 5 working days.
