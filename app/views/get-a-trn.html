Request a teacher reference number (TRN) for a national professional qualification (NPQ)

You need a teacher reference number(TRN) to do an NPQ, even if you’re not a teacher.

If you do not have one, you can request one. Before you do this, check if you have one already using Find a lost TRN.

To request a TRN you need to send a secure email to the Department for Education (DfE) with some personal details and proof of identity.

Follow these steps to do this.

1. Create a Galaxkey account

Galaxkey is a secure email platform. You need to create a Galaxkey account to send a secure email to DfE.

Create your Galaxkey account with an email address that you will not lose access to - such as a personal email address. DfE will respond to this email address with your TRN.

2. Compose an email in Galaxkey

Compose an email in Galaxkey to qts.enquiries@education.gov.uk with the subject line ‘Request TRN for NPQ’.

Include the following information in the body of the email.

  • full legal name, and any previous names if applicable
  • date of birth
  • National Insurance number, if you have one
  • address, if you live outside of the UK
  • NPQ provider

Attach a photo or scanned copy of your proof of identity. You can use one of the following:

  • passport
  • driving licence (full or provisional)
  • certificate of residence
  • birth certificate

3. Send the email

Once you’ve sent the email, DfE will respond within 5 working days.
